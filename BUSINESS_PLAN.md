# Soul Lens — Event Photography & Organization
## Business Plan 2025–2026

---

## Executive Summary

**Soul Lens** is a Vienna-based event photography and organization service specializing in capturing authentic moments at weddings, corporate events, cultural celebrations, and private gatherings. We combine professional photography with end-to-end event coordination to deliver seamless, memorable experiences.

**Mission**: To transform ordinary events into extraordinary memories through artistic photography and meticulous organization.

**Vision**: To become Vienna's most trusted name for premium event services by 2027.

---

## Market Analysis

### Industry Overview
- Austrian event industry valued at ~€2.5B annually
- Vienna accounts for ~40% of Austria's event market
- Growing demand for bundled photography + planning services
- Post-pandemic surge in intimate, high-quality events

### Target Market
| Segment | Description | Priority |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Weddings** | Couples seeking premium, artistic coverage | High |
| **Corporate Events** | Companies hosting conferences, galas, product launches | High |
| **Cultural Events** | Festivals, art exhibitions, museum openings | Medium |
| **Private Celebrations** | Birthdays, anniversaries, milestone parties | Medium |
| **Nonprofit/Government** | Fundraisers, official functions | Low |

### Competitive Landscape
- **Photography-only studios**: High competition, price pressure
- **Full-service planners**: Established but expensive
- **Our edge**: Bundled offering at accessible price point + local Vienna expertise

### Venue Booking Tips for Clients
- **Lead time**: Premium venues require 3–6 months advance booking
- **Peak season**: May–September (weddings), October–December (corporate galas)
- **Off-peak discounts**: January–March (15–30% savings)
- **Weekday savings**: Monday–Thursday bookings often 20–40% cheaper
- **Combined packages**: We negotiate venue + photography bundles for clients

## Legal & Administrative

### Business Structure
- **Type**: Einzelunternehmen (Sole Proprietorship) or GmbH (LLC) — consult Steuerberater
- **Registration**: Gewerbeanmeldung at Magistrat Wien
- **Tax**: Umsatzsteuer (VAT) registration if revenue > €35,000/year
- **Insurance**: Berufshaftpflicht (Professional Liability), Betriebshaftpflicht (Business Liability)

### Required Permits
- Gewerbeschein for photography services
- Event organization may require additional permits for public events
- Music/entertainment licenses for certain venues
